A multitask tool

Daily mobility data is increasing but it may be difficult to handle and use.
Mobiliscope is an open and interactive platform intended to help large audiences to explore cities and their temporal rhythms.
Made for diverse audiences: Mobiliscope is all together a planning tool for policymakers, a scientific tool for researchers, and also a pedagogical tool for teachers and students.

Whatever your profile, you can also play with the Mobiliscope just for fun !
